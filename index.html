#001-Layout Design

magazine-iam

School design project. I conceptualized it as the inaugural issue of a continuing magazine series, selecting blue as the thematic color for this edition. The layout features a text-dominant design, with a specific emphasis on Chinese typography.

#002-visual strategy

poster narrative

A school design project that required the creation of four posters to form a self-statement (or a personal narrative). I approached this task by employing deconstruction, sequence, and recombination, strategically pairing the four pieces to construct my own narrative structure and flow.

#003-street photography

crowd

2025 上海 Shanghai
China's shared bike phenomenon took off and rapidly reached saturation after 2018. Today, various brands of shared bicycles are arguably the most widely used form of transport on Chinese urban streets. In this series, I photographed them specifically to represent and signify the flux of human traffic and crowds.

#004-street photography

intermezzo

2025 上海 Shanghai
The street is a stage—that is my conviction. Consequently, all others become the actors, and I am the audience. This body of work marks the beginning of a larger series, aiming to incorporate and record what is observed through this distinct spectator's perspective.
